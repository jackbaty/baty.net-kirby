I'm having thoughts of backing away from being online. I'm not talking about some crazy "digital detox" nonsense, but rather a return to sanity when it comes to my time online. Related to that are thoughts about sharing photographs. Sometimes I take photos just to have something to share. Why do I do that? Photos should be for me, first. I don't have a real plan, yet, but I'm working on it.

I haven't shot any large format since my film supply was ruined by water in a power outage ice melting incident. I miss it a little. Large format is an expensive pain in the ass there are a million ways to screw it up. It's also cool and the results are uniquely wonderful. Here's a fun self-portrait I did when I still had the (link: https://wiki.baty.net/#Linhof%20Master%20Technika text: Linhof).

I'm losing my energy for any tool that displays words like "Upgrade to Premium!" anywhere. It annoys me more than it probably should, but I'll use something else, thanks.
