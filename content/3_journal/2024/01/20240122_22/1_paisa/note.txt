Title: Paisa

----

Text: As a fan of using plain text [Ledger](https://ledger-cli.org/) files for tracking my finances, I can't believe I've never heard of [Paisa](https://paisa.fyi/). It's essentially a wrapper around a stock Ledger file. There's an editor and really nice dashboards/reporting pages. Definitely worth a look if you're into that sort of thing.
